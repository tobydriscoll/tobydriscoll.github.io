Meet the organisers

  • Toby Driscoll, University of Delaware
  • Endre Süli, University of Oxford
  • Helen Taylor, University of Oxford
  • Alex Townsend, MIT

Toby Driscoll

Toby was a PhD student at Cornell University under the supervision of Nick Trefethen. He has worked with Trefethen on Schwarz-Chrisoffel mapping and is a key contributor to the Chebfun project.

Endre Süli

Endre has been a close colleague of Nick's at Oxford for over 17 years.

Helen Taylor

Helen works very hard keeping everything running smoothly in the Oxford Numerical Analysis Group. She is also the conference secretary.

Alex Townsend

Alex was a PhD student at Oxford University under the supervision of Nick Trefethen. He has worked with Nick on a 2D extension of Chebfun.
