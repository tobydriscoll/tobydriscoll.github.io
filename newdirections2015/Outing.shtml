Conference Excursion

During the conference there will be an afternoon excursion on 26th August 2015. This could be to Blenheim palace, which is an idyllic World Heritage Site in Oxfordshire situated on 2000 acres of parkland. According to wikipedia it was home to the 11th Duke and Duchess of Marlborough and the birthplace of Sir Winston Churchill.

Getting to Blenheim Palace

Blenheim Palace is a 20 minute bus journey away from the city centre of Oxford. A coach will be provided. More details to come.
