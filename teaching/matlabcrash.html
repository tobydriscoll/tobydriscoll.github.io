SIAM (the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics) will be publishing Learning MATLAB, an updated and improved version of the "Crash course in MATLAB", in spring or summer 2009. It will be available in print and as an e-book. Pricing is unknown at this writing, but a major goal has been to keep the length and price appropriate for a supplemental textbook.

This book represents the evolution of a MATLAB cheat sheet that grew to a hundred pages over a period of more than ten years. For all of that time, I have made various incarnations of the "Crash course" available on the web for free. However, SIAM is rightfully concerned about eating into new sales, and they have requested that I no longer make the "Crash course" available on this site. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

Toby Driscoll
