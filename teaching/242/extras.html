Exam help

Some generic advice:

Incorporate at least one simulated exam into your study time. Copy down 5 or 6 problems from the old exams and the homework. Then put everything away and work alone, in peace, for 75 minutes, doing the best you can when you do not know the answer.

If you can get 80% right for one problem type but only 10% of another problem type, you are strategically better off working to get up to 50% on the weakness than being a perfectionist on the strength.
